# YouTube Video Configuration Guide

## How to Add Working YouTube Videos

To replace video IDs with ones that work:

1. Go to YouTube and find a relevant video
2. Copy the video URL: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID_HERE`
3. Extract the VIDEO_ID (the part after `v=`)
4. Replace the videoId in `main.py` with your VIDEO_ID

## Example:
If URL is: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ`
Then videoId is: `dQw4w9WgXcQ`
